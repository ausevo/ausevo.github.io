About AES

Advancing evolutionary biology in Australasia

The Australasian Evolution Society is a learned society founded in 1999, devoted to bringing evolutionary biologists together to promote intellectual exchange and to foster and advance evolutionary biology across the region.

Our main event is our Annual Conference, where researchers come together from across Australasia to present and discuss the latest findings in evolutionary research. Membership allows you to participate and connect with fellow evolutionary biologists.
