## About the Conference

**Evolution in the Gong** — for the first time, the **Australasian Evolution Society (AES)** and the **Society of Australian Systematic Biologists (SASB)** are joining forces in one place.

We'll be gathering at the stunning **University of Wollongong** campus, nestled between the rainforests of the Illawarra Escarpment and the Pacific Ocean, from **2–4 December 2026**.

## Keeping it Affordable

Thanks to the generous [support](/sponsors) of our sponsors, we have worked hard to keep registration fees as low as possible. Whether you're a student, an ECR, or a seasoned researcher, we want cost to be as small a barrier as possible.

## Travel Grants & Awards

Travel grants and ECR plenary awards from both societies will be announced very soon. If you win a grant or award, you will be reimbursed after registering — so don't let the wait for outcomes stop you from registering and missing out on early bird rates!

## Inclusion & Accessibility

We aim to provide a safe, inclusive, and welcoming environment for researchers at all career stages. We warmly encourage participation from students, ECRs, parents, and those from traditionally under-represented groups.

If you have any accessibility requirements or concerns, please don't hesitate to [contact us](mailto:ausevolutionsociety@gmail.com).
