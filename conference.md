### About AES Conferences

The AES Annual Conference is our flagship event, bringing evolutionary biologists together from across Australasia each year. The conference features:

- Plenary talks from leading researchers
- ECR plenary speakers selected from our [Best Paper Awards](/2025_research_awards)
- Student and ECR flash/standard talk sessions
- Workshops and networking events
- Conference dinner

Conference registration is competitively priced, with generous subsidies for students and ECRs.
